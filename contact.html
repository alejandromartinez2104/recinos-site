Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

What types of products do you import?

We import a wide range of products including consumable goods (food, beverages, household items), industrial equipment, electronics, construction materials, and specialty items based on customer needs.

How long does shipping typically take?

Shipping times vary depending on the origin country and product type. Typically, we see 2-4 weeks for standard shipments and 1-2 weeks for express orders. We provide tracking information for all shipments.

Do you offer custom import services?

Yes! We specialize in custom import orders for businesses. Whether you need specific products, bulk quantities, or regular supply arrangements, our team can help source and import exactly what you need.

What are your payment terms?

We offer flexible payment terms including cash on delivery, credit terms for established customers, and various payment methods including bank transfers, credit cards, and financing options for larger orders.
